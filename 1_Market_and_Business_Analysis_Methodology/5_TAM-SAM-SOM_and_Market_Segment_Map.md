# TAM-SAM-SOM 과 Market Segment Map

## 1. 시장 규모 분석의 세 가지 층위

[cite_start]**TAM, SAM, SOM: "시장 크기를 정량적으로 파악하는 계층적 도식"** [cite: 46]

---

### [cite_start]**TAM (Total Addressable Market)** [cite: 51]
* (Total number of potential customers) [cite_start]x (Average annual revenue per customer) [cite: 52]

### [cite_start]**SAM (Serviceable Available Market)** [cite: 53]
* [cite_start]= (Total number of potential customers) x (Percentage of potential customers that can be realistically served) x (Average annual revenue per customer) [cite: 54]

### [cite_start]**SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)** [cite: 55]
* [cite_start]= (Total number of potential customers) x (Percentage of potential customers that can be realistically served by your business) x (Percentage of the market that your business can realistically capture) x (Average annual revenue per customer) [cite: 56]
